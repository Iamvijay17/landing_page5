ABOUT US

A clothing brand for your nomadic soul, rooted in rich Indian traditions. Like all things we love, Bohemian Dream was born from a whim, nurtured by passion, and grew from the unexpected, unpredictable twists that life has gifted us along the way.
We've taken pains to visit nooks of every part of India and partnered with some of the most skilled weavers and artisans to bring you the best of handloom work. Every product of ours tells a story that has been told for generations, a treasure trove of rich traditions and exemplary craftsmanship.
Let’s together look into the future of Indian artisan work, while remembering its glorious past.
OUR HEART LIES IN DESIGN
We work to bring you the classical weaves and art in the best of contemporary designs at par with the modern day trends. Don’t buy just because of handloom. Buy because you have fallen in love with it.
Our style is a bit like a river carving its way through the earth; even when we touch upon a trend, we carry it somewhere new. We create for the woman that’ll make our styles her own. For the woman who wants to make a statement without saying a word. For all the layers of you.
